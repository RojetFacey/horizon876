Twelve Pillar
(Chapter 4-7 Summary)

Twelve pillars’ chapters 4-7 depicts a father son relationship between Charlie and Michael. Their bond grew slowly as they would meet up to discuss Mr. Davis's Pillars of Success. At each meeting Charlie's would teach Michael a few meaningful life lessons that can heavily change his life tremendously. Charlie has a good heart and tries his best to encourage Michael to be cautious of the paths he chooses to follow, manage his time better and not become a slave to the urgent, but be mindful of the relationships you keep with your peers, and last but not least become a lifelong learner.

The lessons thought throughout the chapters have challenge me to actually give my future goals some more thought. Design a plan that better maps out the path I would like to take, to achieve the goals I have set to accomplish. Also, to work on my time management skills in order to not become a slave to the urgent, because truth be told I procrastinate a lot. To analyse and categorize my relationships accordingly to avoid the cons of bad influences. Most of all be mindful of the three important mainstays mentioned which are: reading books, observing, and learning from a successful person and to constantly reflect on one’s own experience learning what went right from wrong. Because formal education makes a living, but self-education makes a fortune.
